Design notes

Screen 2 (Active Set) is the hero screen. Big rep number, ring fills as you go, motivational text triggers when cadence drops. This is what you see at the gym — it needs to be readable in a glance mid-rep.
Screen 3 (HR Rest Timer). The "Ready →" button is dimmed while HR is above target. It goes full-brightness green when HR drops to recovery threshold. User can also tap early to override.
Screen 1 uses a scroll list (Digital Crown) — not all muscles fit on one screen. Scroll to find more. Crown = natural navigation on Watch.
No navigation bar. Every screen is full-bleed. Use swipe-left to go back or Digital Crown to scroll. Keep tap targets ≥44pt.
